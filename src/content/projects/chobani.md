> **Project Summary**
>
> This project explores two Chobani packaging directions: an indulgent Greek yogurt line and a whole-milk concept. The work starts with mood and category research, moves into sketch-based packaging studies, and then tests how premium ingredients, transparency, and dairy-category cues can become clearer shelf-ready systems.
>
> **Project Timeline**
>
> - **Chobani Indulgence**  
>   Build a premium visual language through Mediterranean cues, dessert ingredients, color studies, and mockup iterations.
> - **Whole Milk Strategy**  
>   Study category references, competitor positioning, structural details, and final package directions for a health-minded whole-milk line.

## Chobani Indulgence
The indulgence direction begins with atmosphere. Mediterranean light, ceramic patterning, polished tableware, cherries, chocolate, and soft metallic finishes create a visual world that feels elevated without losing warmth or appetite appeal.

## Indulgence Package Direction
